WHAT WE DO

This is how we help ambitious companies succeed.

We take pride in bringing together a broad spectrum of some of Yale's most talented, motivated, and ambitious entrepreneurs and students to challenge real-world problems in software, healthcare, and biotech.

Product Building

From ideation to prototypes and product building, we help accelerate software development, whether it be a site for the business or building out a software product

Business Research

Research on competitors, existing incumbents, and existing business models to best position your startup for successful funding rounds and market entry.

Branding

We develop distinctive visual identities that communicate startups to VC's, investors, and potential partners

Community

Join a group united by shared passion for healthcare innovation and diverse expertise. Meet our founders, our advisors.
